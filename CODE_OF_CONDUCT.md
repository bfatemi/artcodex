# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Focus disagreement on ideas and observable behavior.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's private information.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and repair harm when mistakes happen.

Harassment, threats, sexualized attention, discriminatory language, deliberate
intimidation, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when conduct is inappropriate, threatening, offensive,
or harmful. Report concerns privately using the maintainer contact method shown
on the repository owner's GitHub profile. Reports will be handled as promptly
and confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing
the project. It is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
